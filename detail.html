About Films & Initiatives

This short 7-minute film tells a story of two lovebirds who finds a lot of new things from behind the window and ends in a certainty of their feelings.

This initiative is a response to the postponement of film production due to the COVID-19 pandemic. As a production company affected, we are moved to keep moving forward. Not only that, through this initiative we also want to invite friends to stay productive by making films together from their respective homes. We want to show that this situation will not limit our creativity to work.

Creative Process

We provide links to our social media followers. With this link, they can send footage of windows in their respective homes. We do not limit the creativity with certain rules, except footage must be related to window, both the situation behind or outside the window. During the 7 day footage collection period, we received 278 window footage from 167 senders.

After looking at the footage, we look for various possible stories. For almost a whole week, Studio Antelopeâ€™s internal team exchanged ideas that are closest to each of our experiences. After the story and dialogue script were finished, we invited two talented young actors, Alyssa Abidin and Jourdy Pranata, to play the two characters in this short film. So in other words, all the processes that took around three weeks were fully carried out from our respective homes.
